Royal Beauty

Jaipur

Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is a captivating destination that blends rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture, making it a must-visit for travelers seeking an authentic Indian experience. The cityâ€™s iconic pink-hued buildings, a tradition dating back to 1876 to welcome the Prince of Wales, create a visually striking and cohesive urban landscape that reflects its deep-rooted heritage and royal legacy

Udaipur

The city's architectural marvels are a major draw, with iconic landmarks such as the City Palace, a 450-year-old palace complex housing museums, courtyards, and gardens, and the floating Jag Mandir Palace located in the middle of Lake Pichola. Other notable attractions include Saheliyon Ki Bari, a lush garden with fountains and marble pavilions, and the Monsoon Palace (Sajjangarh), which offers panoramic views of the city and surrounding hills. The Vintage Car Museum and Crystal Gallery showcase the royal heritage and artistic craftsmanship of the region

Jodhpur

Beyond its forts and colorful streets, Jodhpur boasts significant landmarks such as the white marble Jaswant Thada, a serene mausoleum with intricate carvings and a peaceful lakeside setting, and the opulent Umaid Bhawan Palace, one of the largest private residences in the world, which now serves as a luxury heritage hotel and museum showcasing vintage cars and royal artifacts. The Mandore Gardens, located just outside the city, offer a lush retreat with cenotaphs, temples, and a glimpse into the former capital of the Marwar Kingdom.
